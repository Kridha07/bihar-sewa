# Bihar Sewa

## Trusted Local Services Platform for Bihar

Bihar Sewa is a Bihar-focused local services platform that connects customers with verified local workers including electricians, plumbers, AC repair technicians, carpenters, RO repair experts, and more.

The platform is designed specifically for Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities with a Hindi-first and trust-focused experience.

---

# Vision

To build Bihar’s most trusted local services network by organizing the unorganized workforce through technology, verification, and digital booking.

---

# Problem Statement

In many cities across Bihar, people still rely on:

* random phone numbers
* WhatsApp forwards
* local referrals
* unverified workers

This creates problems like:

* trust issues
* poor service quality
* fake pricing
* delayed services
* difficulty finding skilled workers

Bihar Sewa aims to solve these problems through a verified worker ecosystem.
